F.A.Q

  • Who should attend this event?

    This event is first and foremost for deliverers of digital products and services, as well as those who oversee their delivery. Nerds assemble!

  • Am I guaranteed a ticket if I apply?

    There’s no limit on numbers, but we’ll have a look to make sure you’re in the right place (e.g. that you work for the MOD) before signing you up!

  • What if I find out I can't make it on the day?

    That's ok! If you could let us know, so that you don't receive a feedback form erroneously, that would be helpful.
